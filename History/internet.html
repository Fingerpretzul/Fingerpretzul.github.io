Fun Facts

  • Utah was among one of the first computers ever hooked up to the internet! When the first computers were hooked up to (what was then called the ARPANET) in 1969, the first 4 "nodes" were
    1. University of California, Los Angeles
    2. Augmentation Research Center, California
    3. University of California, Santa Barbara
    4. University of Utah School of Computing, Salt Lake City
    This ARPANET is what would later be the basis for the Internet as we know it today, and the University of Utah was one of the first! Sorry BYU fans.
